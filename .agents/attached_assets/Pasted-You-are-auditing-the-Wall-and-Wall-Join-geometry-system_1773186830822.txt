You are auditing the **Wall and Wall Join geometry system** of a BIM engine.

## Objective

Investigate why **2-wall junctions are not always producing a clean single-point intersection in plan view**, resulting in imperfect joins or multiple vertical seams.

The expected behaviour is:

• Any intersection between **two walls** must produce **one single intersection point in plan (X/Z)**
• Both walls must share that same vertex
• The resulting geometry must produce **one single vertical seam (one Z line)**
• This must work **for any angle** (90°, acute, obtuse, reflex angles)

The current join logic is implemented in a **WallJoinResolver** system using:

* centreline intersection
* tangent computation
* bisector miter plane
* endpoint snapping

However, the join is **not consistently clean**, so a full audit is required.
