Recommended Resources dropdown structure

Based on your codebase, two categories make sense — mirroring the Figma pattern of "GET STARTED" and "LEARN":

**GET STARTED**

- **Quick start guide** — walks through creating your first project, adding a level, placing a wall. Draws from the WelcomeModal onboarding flow. This content exists implicitly; it just needs a page.
- **FAQ** — the 6 questions already in the PricingPage `renderFAQ()`. Pull them into their own standalone page.
- **Keyboard shortcuts** — Ctrl+S (save), plus BIM workspace tool shortcuts. Derivable from the codebase in an afternoon.

**LEARN**

- **AI command reference** — this one is fully ready. `src/ai/Documentation.md` is a complete, structured document covering all query types, batch operations, and spatial queries. It just needs to be presented as a page.
- **IFC compatibility** — your PricingPage FAQ already states: "PRYZM exports IFC2x3 and IFC4, compatible with Revit, ArchiCAD, BIMcollab, Solibri." Expand that one answer into a short guide.
- **AI approval workflow** — how proposals work (you propose, review, approve). The `AIApprovalStore.ts` and `Documentation.md` together give you all the content.

**CONTACT / SUPPORT** (bottom of dropdown, simpler)

- **Contact support** — `mailto:hello@pryzm.io`, already used in multiple places
- **Enterprise & bespoke** — links to the Stream 2 section you're adding to the pricing page
