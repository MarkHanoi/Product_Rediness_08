PROBLEM

Currently slabs are positioned incorrectly.

The slab's BOTTOM face is aligned with the level elevation, which is incorrect BIM behaviour.

Current behaviour:

Level
──────
(bottom of slab)

This means the slab sits above the level, which is wrong.

REQUIRED BIM BEHAVIOUR

The BIM system must follow the architectural convention:

Level = Finished Floor Level (FFL)

Therefore:

Slab behaviour

The TOP of the slab must align with the level elevation, not the bottom.

Correct behaviour:

LEVEL (FFL)
────────────  ← top of slab

████████████  slab thickness

(bottom of slab moves downward)

Meaning:

topOfSlab = levelElevation + baseOffset
bottomOfSlab = topOfSlab - slabThickness

The slab grows downward from the level.

This ensures that when finishes are added later, the structural slab automatically adjusts without breaking wall alignment.

WALL BEHAVIOUR (MUST REMAIN UNCHANGED)

Walls must continue to behave as follows:

Wall base = Level elevation

Meaning:

wallBaseY = BimManager.getLevelElevation(levelId) + baseOffset

Walls start at the level, not at the slab surface.

Do not change wall behaviour.

Walls must remain aligned to the level datum.
