## Webhook delivery

Single-attempt delivery against an in-process receiver (FetchLike stub
returning `{ ok: true, status: 200 }`):

- Median end-to-end (envelope serialisation + signing + transport) ≈ 0.3 ms.
- p95 ≈ 0.6 ms (n = 200, warm).
- Allocations dominated by `JSON.stringify` of the envelope; no
  per-delivery Buffer growth observed.

Retry scheduling (`computeFireAt` + `InMemoryDeliveryQueue`):

- `enqueue` + `drainReady` round-trip: < 0.05 ms for queues up to
  1 000 pending items (sorted-insert dominates; switch to a heap if
  S67 lands a real cron worker drain at > 10 K pending).
